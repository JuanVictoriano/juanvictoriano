Areas of Concern

●Quote & QuoteLineItem are completely unused — 0 records across 77 fields (13 custom). Evaluate whether the Quotes feature should be disabled or removed from the org entirely.
●Account has 57 unused custom fields (0% populated) out of 136 custom fields — 42% of custom Account fields carry no data and are candidates for deletion.
●Opportunity has 30 unused custom fields (0% populated) out of 143 custom fields — 21% of custom Opportunity fields are empty despite 55,654 records.
●Org-wide avg field population is critically low. Account (7.5%), Contact (9.5%), and Opportunity (20.0%) show severe under-utilization — most fields hold data for fewer than 1 in 10 records.
●86 text fields should be picklists across Account (42), Contact (25), Opportunity (17), OLI (1), Product (1). Free text in fields like Company Revenue, Company Staff Count, City, State, and Department reduces reporting accuracy.
●15 duplicate field labels on Account — "Account Type" (2×), "Birthdate" (2×), "Buyer Tier" (2×), "Contact Role" (2×), "Country" (2×), "Department" (2×), "Do Not Call" (2×), "State" (2×), and more. This causes user confusion and reporting errors.
●Product2 has 47% empty fields — 15 of 32 fields (including 6 custom) have no data. With only 192 records, this object needs a data enrichment review.
●Opportunity has 87% custom fields (143 of 164). This extreme customization level increases maintenance complexity and technical debt significantly.
●Only 3 Auto Number / unique identifier fields exist across all objects — Account_ID__c, Opp_Product_ID__c, QuoteNumber. Other objects lack unique external IDs for integration integrity.
●Contact has 15 unused custom fields — lower severity than Account, but still contributing to schema bloat across 1.39M records.
